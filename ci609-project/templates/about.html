Prediction Algorithm

The {prediction algorithm} uses artificial intelligence to analyse team statistics and recent performances to estimate the most likely outcome of a football match. It has been trained using real match data from the top five European leagues.

By identifying patterns in both long-term performance and short-term trends, the {prediction algorithm} is able to produce more accurate outcomes than simple win-loss comparisons.

How It's Used

To use the {prediction algorithm}, simply choose a home team and an away team. The system will evaluate their recent form and historical results, then provide a predicted match outcome.

Logged-in users can save their predictions, export them as a text file, and manage their prediction history. Guests can use the tool freely, but saving and exporting features are only available to registered users.

Privacy

Only the essential information required to manage accounts and save predictions is collected. Passwords are securely encrypted, and no personal data is ever shared or used outside the application.

The {prediction algorithm} does not use user data to improve its model. All data remains private, and users can delete their saved predictions or account information at any time.
